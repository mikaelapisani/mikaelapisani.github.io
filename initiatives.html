I am part of different initiatives that promote sharing knowdlege as well as gender equity and motivation for children and teens in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics).

With ANT we developed BIOFEEDBACK project, an artistic exhition and we have a program called ANT4Kids, which consists of workshops for teachers, children and families on neuroscience and microscopy.
